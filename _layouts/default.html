I am a senior undergraduate student, currently pursuing a Bachelor in Computer Science at the Department of Computer Science and Engineering, University of Minnesota.

During my time at UMN, I've been a part of Multiple Autonomous Robotic Systems (MARS) Laboratory where I'm a research assistant under the supervision of Prof. Stergios Roumeliotis (supported by NSF-IIS). I also had the honor of working with Prof. Hyun Soo Park and Prof. Lana Yarosh on various computer vision and human-computer interaction research projects. On top of that, I spent an amazing summer at Enfusion Systems as a software engineering intern.

I'm interested in computer vision and machine learning. Most of my research is about visual scene understanding, i.e. inferring the physical world from images.
